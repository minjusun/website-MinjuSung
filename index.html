United Nations Fellowship

Contract Management Application and Automation

During my fellowship at the United Nations, I collaborated with an incredible team of developers and strategists to build an application that optimizes the donor contract management for the Mine Action Service within the Department of Peace Operations. My contributions included developing an AI-powered contract parser, designing systems for managing contract data, and supporting preparations for deployment across the organization. This experience strengthened both my technical skills and my ability to work on large scale, mission driven projects.
